Emergency Kit Preparation

Before evacuating, ensure you have an emergency kit prepared. Here are some essentials:

  • Water (1 gallon per person per day for at least three days)
  • Non-perishable food (at least a three-day supply)
  • First aid kit
  • Flashlight with extra batteries
  • Whistle to signal for help
  • Local maps

Evacuation Tips

  • Always have a pre-planned evacuation route in mind.
  • Stay informed about the latest updates and roadblocks.
  • Help others in need during the evacuation.
  • Carry essentials like water, food, and medical supplies.
